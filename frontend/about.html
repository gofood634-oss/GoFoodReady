About Go Food

Your trusted destination for delicious meals delivered fast

Go Food started as a small cloud kitchen aiming to deliver restaurant-quality meals across the city. Our mission is to make delicious food accessible to everyone, everywhere, anytime.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Go Food began with a simple idea: what if you could get restaurant-quality meals delivered to your door in under 30 minutes? We partnered with local restaurants and talented chefs to build a platform that connects food lovers with amazing cuisine.

Our Values

  • Quality: We work only with trusted restaurants and quality ingredients
  • Speed: Fast delivery is our promise - 30 minutes or less
  • Reliability: Track your order in real-time and know exactly when it arrives
  • Affordability: Great food shouldn't break the bank - we offer competitive prices
  • Sustainability: We're committed to eco-friendly packaging and practices

What Sets Us Apart

Unlike traditional delivery apps, we partner directly with restaurants to ensure quality control. Our team carefully curates each menu, and we maintain strict hygiene and food safety standards.

Production-Ready Technology

This demo site showcases our frontend structure. To make it production-ready, you'll need:

  • Backend API (PHP/Node.js/Python)
  • Database (MySQL/PostgreSQL) for users, orders, and restaurants
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Admin dashboard for restaurant and order management
  • Real-time delivery tracking system
